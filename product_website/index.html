About the project

Our project aims to provide an open-source cybersecurity tool for users to help protect themselves from phishing email attacks and other email frauds. Phishing is method that cyber criminals use to trick people into giving personal information or click on malicious links or attachments to install malware. Email is an extremely popular attack vector for cybercriminals as it a direct way to attack individuals at extremely low expense. Emails cost nothing to send and address can easily be scraped from the internet or even guessed/generated allowing for huge volumes to be sent. Also, the impersonal nature of email makes it difficult for users to determine if an email is from a legitimate source.
